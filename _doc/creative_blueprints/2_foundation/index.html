The Case For It

Cricket fans deserve more
than a flat score.

The Problem

  • TV-quality broadcast graphics stay on TV. Wagon wheels, win probability meters, and animated run-rate graphs don't follow fans to the web.

  • Score apps show numbers, not stories. No commentary flow, no partnership context, no run-rate trends — just static figures.

  • Fragmented fixture coverage. IPL, ODI, Test, and T20I spread across multiple apps with inconsistent quality and missing team context.

The Solution

  • Broadcast-grade match center — wagon wheel, win probability, run-rate graph, fall of wickets, and partnership details in a premium TV-style interface.

  • Real-time, story-first updates — ball-by-ball commentary and live statistics flowing continuously. No page refresh. Inspired by Star Sports, JioHotstar, Cricbuzz.

  • One platform, every format — ODI, T20I, Test, and IPL fixtures unified in a single dashboard with team colors, player photos, and match status badges.
